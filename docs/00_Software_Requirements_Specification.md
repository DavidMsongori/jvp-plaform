# 1. Executive Summary

JVP Connect is the official digital platform of Jumuiya ya Vijana wa Pwani (JVP). The platform is designed to provide a secure, modern, and scalable digital ecosystem that connects youth across the six Coastal counties of Kenya with leadership opportunities, programs, events, partnerships, resources, and community initiatives.

Beyond serving as the official website of the organization, JVP Connect will function as an integrated management system supporting membership registration, digital payments, communication, event management, leadership administration, reporting, and organizational governance.

The platform will provide different access levels for visitors, registered members, county leadership, departmental administrators, and system administrators, ensuring secure and efficient management of organizational activities.

JVP Connect is intended to become the central digital hub for all services offered by Jumuiya ya Vijana wa Pwani while maintaining transparency, accountability, efficiency, and accessibility.
